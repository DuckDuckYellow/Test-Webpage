About This Blog

I'm Sam, and I write about my Football Manager adventures.

This series follows my journey with Morecambe FC, a club I found in administration and on the brink of collapse. The challenge? Take them from rock bottom to footballing success, one match at a time.

I write detailed narrative stories with tactical breakdowns, transfer sagas, and the emotional rollercoaster that every FM player knows too well. Each article covers a chunk of the save, capturing the key moments and turning points.

I'm currently managing Morecambe FC, taking them from the lower leagues to (hopefully) Premier League glory. Follow along as we make history!
